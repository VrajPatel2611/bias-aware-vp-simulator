## Case 1: The Chest Pain Trap
Patient: Ramesh, 48M, accountant, 3 days chest pain
Correct diagnosis: GERD
Anchor trap: Chest pain middle-aged male → everyone jumps to MI
Key contradictory clues: burning not crushing, worse after meals,
  takes ibuprofen daily (NSAIDs cause GERD), no arm/jaw radiation,
  antacids helped once, father had diabetes not cardiac disease
Required topics: pain_character, meal_relationship, radiation,
  associated_symptoms, medications, family_history, 
  duration, relieving_factors
Minimum questions: 7
